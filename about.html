About Us

With an Expanse of Experience

Spanning over two decades, Jumpstart Services Inc./Jumpstart Care Inc. emerges as the brainchild of passionate therapists who share a common vision: to provide evidence-based, professional skilled therapy interventions. Our journey is underscored by an unwavering commitment to functional outcomes, holistic wellness, and the seamless continuum of care for each and every patient we are privileged to serve.

A Foundation of Excellence

Built on a foundation of excellence, our ethos revolves around cultivating a team of exceptional therapists. We champion integrity and compassion as core values, ensuring that every therapeutic encounter is marked by the highest quality of care and meticulously documented services. Our aspiration is to not just meet expectations, but to consistently exceed them.

Our Pledge

We stand resolute in our pledge to maintain the pinnacle of professionalism and service delivery to both our valued clients and cherished patients. This commitment shapes every facet of our operation, fostering an environment where quality thrives.
