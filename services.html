FAQ

What insurance do you take?

We take pride in our commitment to accommodating a diverse array of insurance providers. Our dedication to comprehensive care extends to accepting a broad spectrum of insurance options, allowing us to cater to the varying needs of our valued patients.

What are your qualifications?

Our licensed and insured therapists prioritize strict adherence to HIPAA, MEDICARE, and MEDICAID guidelines. We rigorously comply with state regulations and licensure requirements, emphasizing ethics and professionalism. Our dedicated team consistently delivers high-quality interventions in physical, occupational, and speech therapies, ensuring excellence in care.

Do you accept Credit Cards?

Yes, we gladly accept all major credit cards for your convenience. Please note that a 3% processing fee applies to all credit card transactions to help offset the costs associated with payment processing services. We believe in transparency with our pricing and want to ensure you're fully informed about any additional fees before processing your payment.
